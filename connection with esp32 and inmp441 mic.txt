Driver(L298N) to ESP32
Motor Pairs (Parallel):
Left Front & Left Back motors -> Connect together to OUT1 & OUT2.
Right Front & Right Back motors -> Connect together to OUT3 & OUT4.

Control Pins:
Left Motor: IN1=GPIO 13, IN2=GPIO 12, ENA=GPIO 19 (PWM)
Right Motor: IN3=GPIO 14, IN4=GPIO 27, ENB=GPIO 18 (PWM)

Power (CRITICAL SAFETY):
11.1V Battery (+) to L298N 12V Input.
11.1V Battery (-) to L298N GND.
L298N 5V Output -> ESP32 VIN/5V pin. (Powers the ESP32)
L298N GND -> ESP32 GND. (Common Ground)

ESP32 -------> PCA9685 (Servo Driver)
Step 1: Power the Motors (Green Terminal)
 Buck (+) -> Green Terminal (V+)
 Buck (-) -> Green Terminal (GND/Other Hole)
 Capacitor -> Screwed into Green Terminal (Red to V+, Black to GND)
Step 2: Power the Chip & Signal (Side Pins)
 ESP32 3.3V (or 5V) -> Side VCC (Powers the chip logic)
 ESP32 GND -> Side GND (Common ground)
 ESP32 GPIO 15 -> Side SDA
 ESP32 GPIO 14 -> Side SCL
Step 3: Plug in Servos

Black/Brown wire faces OUT/BOTTOM.

 Left Shoulder -> Port 0
 Left Elbow -> Port 1
 Right Shoulder -> Port 2
 Right Elbow -> Port 3
